Cristiano Ronaldo: The Greatest Footballer

Biography

Cristiano Ronaldo was born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal, a small island off the western coast of the country. By his early teens, Ronaldo's talent and legend had grown considerably. After a stint with Nacional da liha da Madeira, he signed with Sporting Portugal in 2001.

Club Career

Cristiano Ronaldo began his club career at Sporting CP, making his senior debut at age 17. His talent quickly attracted attention from top European clubs, leading to his transfer to Manchester United in 2003, where he flourished under manager Alex Ferguson before embarking on a successful career spanning several clubs and leagues, including a notable stint in Saudi Arabia with Al Nassr.

International Achievements

Over the course of his career, Portuguese footballer Cristiano Ronaldo has received five Ballon d'Or/FIFA Ballon d'Or awards, the most for a European player. Widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time, Ronaldo holds the record for most goals and assists in the UEFA Champions League (140 and 42 respectively), and the record for most goals in the UEFA European Championship (14), its qualification stage (40), and the FIFA Club World Cup (7), as well as most goals scored in a UEFA Champions League season (17 in 2013–14), most international goals (130), and most appearances in a national team (207). He has scored a record 895 senior career goals for club and country. Moreover, he is one of the few recorded players to have made over 1,200 professional career appearances.

Collectively, Ronaldo has won 33 senior trophies in his career. He has also attained one title from youth and at least five titles from friendly competitions. All in all he had won over 300 trophies and medals by January 2021, with some of them dating back to his childhood.

Personal Life

Cristiano Ronaldo has five children. His eldest, a son born in June 2010, resides with him under an agreement protecting the mother's privacy. Following his relationship with Irina Shayk, Ronaldo became a father to twins via surrogacy in 2017. He is currently in a relationship with Georgina Rodríguez, with whom he welcomed a daughter in November 2017 and experienced the loss of one of a pair of expected twins during childbirth in 2022.
